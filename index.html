Layer 1 · Unaided recall
500 identical calls per model: "List every French bank you know." Establishes parametric share of mind and defines each model's bank universe.
Universe selection
Each model's universe = its own top-recalled banks. Sizes differ by design — Gemini 10, GPT-4o 20 — because the models hold different depths of knowledge. That asymmetry is itself a finding.
Layer 2 · Territory association
One bank per prompt, 50 calls per bank: "Pick the top 3 service categories you associate with this bank." Near-100% parse success, no truncation.
Matrix & analysis
Association score 0–100 per bank × service = share of the 50 calls assigning that territory. Surfaces monopolies, contested zones, and white space.
